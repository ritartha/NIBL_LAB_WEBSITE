About NIBL

Nuclear Instrumentation & Beam Laboratory

The Nuclear Instrumentation & Beam Laboratory (NIBL) is a state-of-the-art research facility dedicated to advancing nuclear physics research and ion beam applications.Our laboratory houses a sophisticated 400 KeV accelerator system designed for precision beam generation and analysis.

Research Focus Areas:

  • Ion beam physics and applications
  • Nuclear instrumentation development
  • Material characterization using ion beams
  • Beam diagnostic systems
  • Accelerator technology research

Facilities:

  • 400 KeV Ion Accelerator
  • Advanced beam diagnostic systems
  • Real-time data acquisition and logging
  • Professional laboratory environment
  • Collaborative research spaces
