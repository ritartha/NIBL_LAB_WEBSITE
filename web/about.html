Our Mission

To advance accelerator physics research and ion beam analysis work through cutting-edge ion beam technology, fostering scientific innovation and training the next generation of nuclear scientists.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in scientific research on ion beam physics, contributing to global scientific knowledge and technological advancement.

Laboratory Overview

The Nuclear Instrumentation & Beam Laboratory (NIBL) is a state-of-the-art research facility dedicated to advancing nuclear physics research and ion beam applications.Our laboratory houses a sophisticated 400 KeV accelerator system designed for precision beam generation and analysis.

Established with a commitment to excellence in nuclear research, NIBL serves as a hub for interdisciplinary collaboration, bringing together researchers from physics, materials science, and engineering to tackle complex scientific challenges.

Research Focus Areas

Ion Beam Physics

Advanced studies in ion-matter interactions, beam dynamics, and particle acceleration physics.

Nuclear Instrumentation

Development of innovative detection systems, measurement techniques, and diagnostic tools.

Accelerator Technology

Research in beam acceleration methods, system optimization, and performance enhancement.

Materials Analysis

Ion beam analysis for material characterization, surface modification, and thin film studies.
